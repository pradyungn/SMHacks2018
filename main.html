Globally, 4 Billion tons of food are not eaten every year.

In the United States, around 40% of food is eventually thrown away.

The Average household wastes around $2,200 worth of food each year in the US.

What is FoodSaver?

FoodSaver is a free service designed to help families keep track of the food they have so that they can save money and food. FoodSaver will:

  • Help families keep track of what food they already have even if it is is hidden
    in the depths of their fridge.
  • Send reminders to eat food before it begins to go bad.
  • Suggest recipies for new meals made from the excess food in their pantry.
