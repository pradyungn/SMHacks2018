What is Foodsaver?

About 40% of food bought in the United States is never used. This equates to around $2,200 of food being watsed by each household each year on average. FoodSaver is a free service designed to help solve this problem. All you need to do is sign in and input the foods you get when you buy them. FoodSaver will remind you to use all of the food you bought even if it is hidden in the back of your Fridge and suggest recipies for you to cook with some of the food you may have forgotten about.
